Pad Thai Recipe


Welcome! This is Ryan's famous Pad Thai Recipe. You're going to love it!

This recipe involves a lot of fresh, rich greens, and aromatic garlic, ginger as well as tamarine! Get your wok's ready. It's time to cook!

Ingredients

Here are the things you will need:

  • Cooked Shrimp
  • Dried Noodles
  • Chinese Green Onions
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Maesri's Pad Thai Sauce

How to make Pad Thai

  1. Put dried noodles in boiling water for 5-6mins.
  2. Pray to god.
  3. Start to heat up your wok.
  4. Put in some vegetable oil. Add your Bean Sprouts and Green Onions
  5. Let them cook for 3-4mins
  6. Add your Cooked Shrimp.
  7. Add your noodles. And finally add the Pad Thai Sauce
  8. Be sure to stir noodles. Let them soak up the sauce.
  9. Put it all on a plate. And your finisihed!
